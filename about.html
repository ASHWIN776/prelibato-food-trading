About Us

Prelibato is a food trading business that aims to deliver nothing but the highest quality and most delicious experience.

Prelibato only sources the best ingredients from world-renowned vendors, many of which have been family-owned for generations. We strictly adhere to guidelines set by the Food Inspection Agency for food safety and quality control. From farm to fork, we maintain the highest standards of hygiene.

We take our customers' budget into consideration when buying foods and vegetables. That's why our prices are so competitive, yet we still offer the same high-quality of products that you would find anywhere else.

What good is food that's not fresh? We only use fresh ingredients sourced from the best suppliers around the world. With us, you'll never have to worry about your ingredients again!You can't get any fresher than when it's just been picked from the ground. That's why we work with farmers who take extreme care to harvest produce as soon as possible. It makes for better tasting food, and of course better for us all!

Prelibato has a customer service team on standby 24/7, ready to help you with any question or issue. We take your feedback seriously and provide tailored, personalized responses to your queries.
